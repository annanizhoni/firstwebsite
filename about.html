About


Anna Nizhoni is a multimedia artist who is skilled in a wide variety of mediums, including: charcoal portraiture, beadwork, pinstriping and graphic design. She is currently transitioning into tech after working in the automotive industry as a service writer and emissions technician. Anna is attending MIT's coding bootcamp and Google's UX design certification course while working as a freelance digital marketer in New York City.
